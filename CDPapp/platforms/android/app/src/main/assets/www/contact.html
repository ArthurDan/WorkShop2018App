    Wish to pre-order products?

    At this stage we are unable to pre-order items online. If you'd like to pre-order an item that is 'Coming Soon' then please contact a Città store and they'll help you out.

    Need help placing an order or creating an account?

    The good news is that you don't need an account to buy from our online store. To buy from our online store, add purchases to the shopping cart and go through to the checkout. You can choose to create an acount at this stage, or checkout as a guest. Fill in your details an complete the transaction. For your security, we never store card payment details. If you choose to create an account, on future visits you will be able to login by clicking at the top of the page or by signing in at the checkout.
